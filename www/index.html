Our unique, dynamic and fully automated decision making strategy allows us to trade profitably in any market, without the risk of impending model collapse, even under fast evolving market conditions.

AI Strategy

One of the biggest obstacles to successful, automated trading is model collapse under evolving market conditions. Strategies are fitted to the present dynamics without guarantees of success in the future. Our AI model is vastly different:

Constantly evolves with the market

Self-heals when market dynamics change by re-learning an optimal strategy

Anti-fragile in nature, benefits from uncertainty

Market neutral and able to model any relevant medium time span data series
